YOGA TEACHER, PERFORMANCE ARTIST, & TRAVELLER

I am so grateful to be supporting Creative Yogis to cultivate a creatively spiritual life for themselves through a combination of Movement & Mindset practices so that their creations are an honest expression of who they are.

After my dad passed away in July 2014 my life flipped upside down. I decided that it was time to start living freely and make the most out of each and every day. It didn't happen over night. I struggled through severe anxiety and negative thoughts cycles galore.

Then I found Yoga. (Cue the Angelic birdsong)

Since then, I have completed 650hrs of teacher training, accumulated 1500+ hours of teaching experience, and spent 2 years living on a beach in India training students to become Yoga Teachers.

Now, my business allows me to live a life of meaning & purpose and constantly experience new things.

I know you have a passion for personal growth and feeling accomplished.

With my support you can cultivate a growth mindset which will give you the mental freedomto develop your creative practice and help and inspire others. This means that you can create your desired income from creations that are an honest expression of who you are.

I can show you the door...but it's up to you to walk through it. I know you can do it...do you?
